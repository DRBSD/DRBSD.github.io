Important Dates

Full Paper submission deadline: August 26, 2022 (AoE)

Author notification: September 12, 2022

Camera-ready final papers submission deadline: September 23, 2022 (AoE)

Submissions

• Papers should be submitted electronically on SC Submission Website.

https://submissions.supercomputing.org

• Paper submission must be in IEEE format.

https://www.ieee.org/conferences/publishing/templates.html

• DRBSD-8 will accept full papers (6 pages, excluding references and appendix), and extended abstracts (2 pages, including references and appendix).

• Submitted papers will be evaluated by at least 3 reviewers based upon technical merits.
