Submissions

• Papers should be submitted electronically on SC Submission Website.

https://submissions.supercomputing.org

• Paper submission must be in IEEE format.

https://www.ieee.org/conferences/publishing/templates.html

• DRBSD-9 will accept full papers (limited to 8 pages excluding references) and extended abstracts (2 pages, except references).

• Submitted papers will be evaluated by at least 3 reviewers based upon technical merits. The accepted papers will be published with IEEE/ACM through IEEE TCHPC

DRBSD-9 encourages submissions to provide artifact description & evaluation. Details: https://sc23.supercomputing.org/program/papers/reproducibility-initiative/
