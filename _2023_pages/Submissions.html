Submissions

• Papers should be submitted electronically on SC Submission Website.

https://submissions.supercomputing.org

• Paper submission must be in IEEE format.

https://www.ieee.org/conferences/publishing/templates.html

• DRBSD-9 will accept full papers (6 pages, excluding references and appendix), and extended abstracts (2 pages, including references and appendix). Appendixes are allowed to take up to 2 pages.

• Submitted papers will be evaluated by at least 3 reviewers based upon technical merits.
